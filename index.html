Short Bio

Dr. Hadeel M. Jawad is currently working as a faculty member in the Computer Science and Engineering Dept. at Oakland University. She had her bachelor's and master's degrees in computer engineering and she earned her Ph.D. from Eastern Michigan University in Technology Management. She also has a Graduate Certificate in Advanced Web and Mobile Application Development from San Diego State University. Her research interest is in gamifying CS education and the best way to teach programming for females and underrepresentation minorities. Her other research interest is in mobile application development, Human-computer Interaction, and she also has a recent paper on information security. She is also interested in creating and exploring different tools that teach programming. During her Ph.D. study, Dr. Jawad created an online tool that integrates art and animation in teaching computer programming to make it more interesting for learners. The tool can be found at theCodeGenie.com
